▌ HOW IT WORKS

  1. Export your GHL contacts as CSV (Contacts → … → Export).
  2. Make sure the CSV has columns named email and phone (case-insensitive). Other columns are ignored.
  3. Upload below. We match each row to an existing Ambassador by email and store the parsed phone (E.164 format) plus country (ISO code).
  4. Idempotent: rows that already have a phone are skipped. Re-uploading the same CSV is safe.
